> ### 常义积分的性质

> $$
> \begin{alignedat}{3}
> \fbox{1}\; & f (x, u), \alpha (u), \beta (u) \in \mathcal{Conti.} \quad\Rightarrow\quad \int_{\alpha (u)}^{\beta (u)} f (x, u) \mathrm{d} x \in \mathcal{Conti.} \\
>
> \end{alignedat}
> $$
