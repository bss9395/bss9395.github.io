> ### 等比恒等式

> $$
> \begin{alignedat}{3}
> 1 - x^n = (1 - x) \cdot \sum_0^{n - 1} x^n \\
> \end{alignedat}
> $$
>

> ### $\imath$ 恒等式

> $$
> \begin{array}{l|l|l}
> \fbox{1}\; & e^{\imath x} = \cos x + \imath \sin x                 & e^{\imath \pi} + 1 = 0 \\
> \fbox{2}\; & \cos x = \dfrac{1}{2} (e^{\imath x} + e^{- \imath x}) & \sin x = \dfrac{1}{2 \imath} (e^{\imath x} - e^{- \imath x}) \\
> \end{array}
> $$
