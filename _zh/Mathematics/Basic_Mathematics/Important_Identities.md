> ### 等比恒等式

> $$
> \begin{alignedat}{3}
> 1 - x^n = (1 - x) \cdot \sum_0^{n - 1} x^n \\
> \end{alignedat}
> $$
>

> ### $\imath$ 恒等式

> $$
> \begin{alignedat}{3}
> \fbox{1}\; & e^{\imath x} = \cos x + \imath \sin x \\
> \fbox{2}\; & e^{\imath \pi} + 1 = 0
> \end{alignedat}
> $$
