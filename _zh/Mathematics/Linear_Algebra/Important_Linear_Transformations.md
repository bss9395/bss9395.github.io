> ### 函数的线性变换

> $$
> \begin{alignedat}{3}
> \fbox{1}\; & g (t) \xlongequal[t \in \lbrack 0, 1 \rbrack; x \in \lbrack \alpha, \beta \rbrack]{x = \alpha + (\beta - \alpha) t} f [\alpha + (\beta - \alpha) t] \\
> \fbox{2}\; & g (x) \xlongequal[g (\beta) = f (\beta)]{g (\alpha) = f (\alpha)} [f (x) - f (\alpha)] - x [f (\beta) - f (\alpha)] \\
> \end{alignedat}
> $$
