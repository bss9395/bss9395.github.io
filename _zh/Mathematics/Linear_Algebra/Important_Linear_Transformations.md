> ### 函数定义域的线性变换

> $$
> \begin{alignedat}{3}
> \fbox{1}\; & f (x) \xlongequal[x \in \lbrack \alpha, \beta \rbrack; t \in \lbrack 0, 1 \rbrack]{x = \alpha + (\beta - \alpha) t} f (\alpha + (\beta - \alpha) t) \\
> \fbox{2}\; & f (t + T) = f (t) \xlongequal[f (\frac{T}{2 \pi} x)]{t = \frac{T}{2 \pi} x} g (x) = g (x + 2 \pi) \\
> \end{alignedat}
> $$
