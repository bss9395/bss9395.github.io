> ### 多项分布 $X \mapsto \mathrm{M_{ultinomial}} (n, p_1, \cdots, p_m)$

> $$
> \begin{alignedat}{3}
> P (X_1 = k_1, \cdots, X_m = k_m) &= \dfrac{n !}{k_1 ! \cdots k_m !} p_1^{k_1} \cdots p_m^{k_m} \quad\Leftarrow\quad n = \sum_1^m k_m \\
> 
> \end{alignedat}
> $$
